About Food & Beverage Nepal

Our Project is all about inspiring cooks of all kinds of dishes from all around the globe. We engage especially Nepali, Chinese, Continental, Indian cuisine which is similar to world cuisine. We make easy recipes so that anyone can learn to cook easily within some minutes. If you enjoy what we are doing our videos, please do not hesitate to give us feedback and if you want to learn all types of dishes, do not forget to subscribe to our youtube channel. And share our videos with your friends and relatives through social media you're using, so that they can also learn to cook delicious food.
